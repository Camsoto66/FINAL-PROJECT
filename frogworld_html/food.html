What Do Frogs Eat?

Most frogs are carnivores. They usually eat small animals they can catch, especially insects. Different frogs eat different things depending on their size and where they live.

Common Foods

  • Insects (flies, crickets, beetles)
  • Worms
  • Snails and slugs
  • Spiders
  • Small fish (for bigger frogs)

Tadpole Food

Tadpoles often start by eating algae and tiny plants in the water. As they grow, some species may begin eating small insects too.

Fun fact: Frogs don’t chew. They swallow food whole! Many frogs use their sticky tongues to catch prey.
