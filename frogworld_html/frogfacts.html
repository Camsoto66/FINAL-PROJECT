Cool Frog Facts

  • There are more than 7,000 species of frogs around the world.
  • Frogs don’t drink water — they absorb it through their skin.
  • Some frogs can jump over 20 times their body length.
  • The glass frog has transparent skin, so you can see its organs.
  • Poison dart frogs are brightly colored to warn predators they are toxic.
  • Frogs start life as tadpoles and go through metamorphosis.
  • Some frogs can freeze solid in winter and survive.

Why Frogs Are Important

Frogs help control insect populations and are an important part of the food chain. Scientists also study frogs because their sensitive skin makes them early warning signs of environmental problems.
